# Marisol Bakery — Brand

Welcome to the Marisol Bakery brand space. Everything here is yours to use: the logo, the
colors, the type, and the way we sound.

## How we sound
Warm, plain, neighborly. We speak the way we'd greet someone at the counter.

## Near-miss copy (must NOT trip the deny-list — these are ordinary brand words)
- Our beans are harvested at dawn and our bread is baked by six.
- We matched the season's palette to the colors of ripe wheat.
- This is the next stage of growth for the bakery, and our hero image leads the page.
- The source of our recipes is three generations of family notes.
- We love hand-drawn touches across the menu and the window signs.
